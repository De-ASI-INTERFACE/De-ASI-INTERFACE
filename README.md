# De-ASI-INTERFACE

**The base layer for decentralized AI, Solana-native agentic finance, and ASI ecosystem interoperability.**

De-ASI-INTERFACE is the interface layer that connects AI agents, Solana DeFi, SPL token workflows, and emerging decentralized AI infrastructure into one production-ready system.

It is designed for builders who need a single control plane for autonomous execution, routing, monitoring, and integration across onchain and offchain systems.

---

## Why This Matters Now

AI agents are moving from chatbots to autonomous economic actors — they own wallets, pay for compute, and execute on-chain strategies without human input. Solana has become a primary venue for machine-to-machine agentic activity because of its speed and growing transaction share. The ASI Alliance vision is to build decentralized artificial superintelligence through open-source infrastructure, unified tooling, and scalable decentralized computing.

De-ASI-INTERFACE sits at the intersection of all three.
