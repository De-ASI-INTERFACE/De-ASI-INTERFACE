## 1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the behavioral and ethical standards expected of all contributors, collaborators, partners, and users interacting with De-ASI-INTERFACE, its affiliated organizations, repositories, platforms, and decentralized systems.

The standards reflected in this policy have governed operations since the founding of De-ASI-INTERFACE on October 22, 2025.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All GitHub repository contributors and collaborators
- External API integrators and partner developers
- Community members on public channels (Discord, X/Twitter, Telegram)
- Internal team members and contractors

---

## 3. Our Standards

### 3.1 Expected Behavior
- Communicate professionally and respectfully at all times
- Provide constructive, evidence-based technical feedback
- Acknowledge intellectual property rights and licensing
- Report security vulnerabilities responsibly via designated channels
- Maintain confidentiality of proprietary algorithms and trading strategies

### 3.2 Prohibited Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or threatening conduct of any kind
- Unauthorized access to systems, wallets, or private repositories
- Front-running, wash trading, or market manipulation using platform tools
- Distribution of proprietary code, trading logic, or API keys without authorization
- Misrepresentation of identity or affiliation

---

## 4. Enforcement

Violations will result in:
1. **Warning** — First offense with documented remediation path
2. **Temporary Suspension** — Removal from repositories and communication channels
3. **Permanent Ban** — For severe or repeated violations including legal referral where applicable

---

## 5. Reporting

Report violations to: **conduct@de-asi-interface.io**  
All reports are confidential and reviewed within 72 hours.
